PCOS is a hormonal disorder that can lead to irregular periods, infertility, weight gain, and metabolic issues. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and lifestyle management.

About the Dataset

We utilized a real-world dataset sourced from Kaggle and validated clinical data. The dataset includes medical and lifestyle features:

  • Hormonal indicators: LH, FSH, PRL, AMH
  • Body and lifestyle metrics: BMI, fast food intake, Vitamin D3
  • Vital signs: BP Systolic and Diastolic
  • Reproductive history: Menstrual cycle length, pregnancy status
The data has been cleaned, normalized, and used to train an accurate ML model.

  • AI-driven Screening: Uses trained ML models to detect potential PCOS patterns.
