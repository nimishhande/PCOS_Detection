PCOS Detection and Awareness

Welcome to the PCOS checker website — helping young women predict Polycystic Ovary Syndrome risk using medical indicators and data science.

This project aims to predict the risk of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) in females aged 13–25 using machine learning techniques. PCOS is a hormonal disorder that can lead to irregular periods, infertility, weight gain, and metabolic issues. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and lifestyle management.
